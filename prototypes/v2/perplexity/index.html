About CityProfiler.ro

CityProfiler.ro helps you compare Romanian cities based on various amenities and lifestyle factors. Our data includes venue statistics across multiple categories to help you make informed decisions about where to live, work, or visit.

How to Use

  • Browse cities in List or Map view
  • Click on any city to see detailed statistics
  • Select up to 3 cities to compare side-by-side
  • Adjust category weights to calculate custom scores
  • Use preset profiles or create your own criteria
